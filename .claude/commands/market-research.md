description: Conduct deep market research for a selected project idea
---

You are conducting comprehensive market research for a mission-driven open-source project.

1. **Ask which project** to research

2. **Research and document:**
   - **Market Size & Growth**
     - TAM/SAM/SOM calculations
     - Growth trends (CAGR)
     - Market drivers

   - **Competitive Landscape**
     - Direct competitors (commercial & open-source)
     - Pricing comparison
     - Feature comparison matrix
     - Competitive advantages/disadvantages

   - **Target Audience**
     - Primary personas (detailed)
     - Secondary personas
     - User pain points
     - Current solutions they use

   - **Business Model**
     - Monetization options
     - Pricing strategy
     - Distribution channels

   - **Go-to-Market Strategy**
     - Launch channels
     - Community building
     - Content strategy
     - Partnership opportunities

3. **Use web search** to find recent data (2024-2025)

4. **Provide recommendations:**
   - Should we pursue this? Why/why not?
   - Biggest opportunities
   - Biggest risks
   - Recommended positioning

5. **Save research** to `RESEARCH_[PROJECT_NAME].md`

Be thorough and data-driven. Include sources and links.
